Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about EventSphere

Booking & Tickets

Payment

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), UPI, net banking, digital wallets (PayPal, Google Pay, Apple Pay), and EMI options for eligible purchases. All payments are processed through secure, razorpay gateway.

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways. We never store your complete card details on our servers. All transactions are processed through secure third-party payment processors with bank-grade security.

Payment declines can happen due to insufficient funds, incorrect card details, bank security restrictions, or transaction limits. Please verify your card information, ensure sufficient balance, and check with your bank. If the issue persists, try using a different payment method or contact your bank.

For Organizers

Click "Become an Organizer" in the footer, fill out the registration form with your organization details, Our team will review your application within 2-3 business days and activate your organizer account.

Payments are processed within 7 business days after your event concludes. The amount is transferred to your registered bank account after deducting service fees and applicable taxes. You can track all payouts in your organizer dashboard under "Financials".

Refunds & Cancellation

Refund policies vary by event and are set by the organizer. Generally, full refunds are available if cancelled 7+ days before the event, 50% if cancelled 3-7 days before, and no refund within 48 hours of the event. Check the specific refund policy on the event page before booking.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days from the date of cancellation. The time for the amount to reflect in your account depends on your bank and payment method - typically 3-5 business days for credit/debit cards and 1-2 days for UPI/digital wallets.
